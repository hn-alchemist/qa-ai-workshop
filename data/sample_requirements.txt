# ---------- Authentication & accounts ----------
As a user, I can log in with a valid email and password.
As a user, I can reset my password via an email link that expires in 15 minutes.
As a user, I can enable two-factor authentication using an authenticator app.
As an admin, I can deactivate a user account, after which that user cannot log in.
As a user, I can update my registered email address and must verify the new one.

# ---------- E-commerce ----------
As a shopper, I can add up to 10 items to my cart and see the running total update.
As a customer, I can apply a discount coupon at checkout; expired coupons are rejected.
As a user, I can pay with a credit card; invalid card numbers are rejected with a clear error.
As a shopper, I can filter products by price range and sort by rating.
As a customer, I can request a refund within 30 days of purchase.

# ---------- Content & files ----------
As a user, I can upload a profile picture (JPG/PNG only, max 5 MB).
As a reader, I can leave a comment (max 500 characters) on an article when logged in.
As a user, I can export my transaction history for a selected date range as a CSV file.
As an editor, I can schedule a blog post to publish at a future date and time.

# ---------- Tricky / ambiguous on purpose (great for discussion) ----------
# These are vague or under-specified. Watch what the AI assumes — and what it misses.
As a user, I want the app to be fast.
Users should be able to search.
The system must handle a lot of traffic.
As a user, I can transfer money to another account.
The form should validate the input properly.

# ---------- High-stakes (think security, money, privacy) ----------
As a user, I can withdraw funds to my linked bank account, up to a daily limit of 1000.
As an admin, I can grant or revoke another user's permissions.
As a user, I can delete my account and all my personal data (GDPR right to erasure).
As a patient, I can view my medical records but cannot edit them.
